NEW REMOTE WORK UPDATE (CONFIDENTIAL)
=====================================

Section 1: Temporary Remote Work Suspension
Due to recent performance audits, the hybrid work model is hereby suspended. Starting next month, all full-time employees are required to work from the corporate office 5 days per week. Remote work is no longer permitted under any circumstances unless a medical exemption is approved by HR.

Section 2: Office Hours Shift
Core collaboration hours are extended. All employees must be physically present at their desks from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST.

Section 3: Home Equipment Return
All home office equipment stipends are discontinued. Employees who received the $500 setup stipend must return any purchased equipment to the IT department by the end of the quarter.
